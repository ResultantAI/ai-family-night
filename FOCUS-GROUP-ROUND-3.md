- **Safety:** Multi-layer content moderation with Grandma Mode override
- **Performance:** All games load <2 seconds, AI responses <5 seconds
- **Browser Support:** Chrome, Edge, Safari (Firefox limited for voice)

**Known Limitations:**
- Voice features require microphone permission
- Audio recording may not work on older devices
- Claude features need internet connection
- API rate limit: 10 requests/minute (should never hit in normal use)

**Privacy:**
- All data stored locally (localStorage)
- No user accounts yet (coming in public beta)
- AI requests logged for safety review
- No third-party analytics or tracking
- COPPA compliant (no data from under 13)
